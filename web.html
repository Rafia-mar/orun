Our Products

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Tools

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) tools are essential for ensuring the integrity and safety of materials and structures without causing damage. Our range includes:

Read More

Ultrasonic Testing (UT): Detect internal defects using high-frequency sound waves.

Eddy Current Testing: Detect surface and near-surface defects in conductive materials.

Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing: Advanced technique for precise and efficient scanning.

Radiographic Testing (RT): Use X-rays or gamma rays to examine internal structures.

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT): Detect surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials.

Dye Penetrant Testing: Detect surface defects in non-porous materials.
